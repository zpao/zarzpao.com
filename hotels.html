Los Gatos

Santa Cruz

We're not setting up any room blocks here, but Santa Cruz is still pretty close to Radonich Ranch. We'd rather you stay closeby so that you can easily be a part of the festivities on Friday and Sunday, but if that's what you want to do...

San Francisco

We really don't suggest staying in San Francisco. You'll be far from the festivities and we wouldn't want you to miss anything!

If you're planning on arriving in the bay area early or sticking around after the wedding, we'd be happy to suggest places to stay.
